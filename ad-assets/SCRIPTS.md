# Poker Monkey — TikTok Ad Scripts & Storyboards

**Product:** 1v1 Texas Hold'em matchmaking — queue up, get paired with a real player, play heads-up, win ELO.
**URL:** https://pokermonkey.app
**Goal:** App installs + virality (shareable hook-driven creative).
**Tone:** Cheeky, pirate-monkey energy. Fast cuts. Loud captions. Never feels like a corporate poker app.

## TikTok ad specs (paid, in-feed)
- **Aspect:** 9:16 vertical, **1080×1920** (all our static images match this).
- **Length:** 9–15s converts best for installs; up to 60s allowed. Hook in **first 2 seconds**.
- **Safe zone:** keep text/logo/CTA out of the **bottom ~480px** and **right ~120px** — TikTok overlays the caption, profile, and action buttons there. (Our static CTAs sit low for organic/Spark use; for paid in-feed, prefer the `*-safe` crops or move CTA up — see README.)
- **Format:** MP4 / MOV, H.264, ≤500MB. Square-ish thumbnails get cropped — design for full-bleed vertical.
- **Sound on:** 90%+ of TikTok plays with sound. Use trending audio for organic/Spark; licensed/!owned audio for paid.
- **No real-money gambling claims.** This is free, ELO-based, no cash payouts. Keep copy to "play / win ELO / climb the rank" — avoid "bet real money", odds, or casino framing (ad-policy + app-store safe).
